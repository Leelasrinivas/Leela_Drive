FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a driving licence for self-drive?

Yes, a valid Indian driving licence is mandatory. International licences are also accepted for foreign nationals visiting Hyderabad.

How far in advance should I book?

We accommodate same-day bookings (subject to availability), but we recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to guarantee your preferred vehicle.

Are all vehicles insured?

Yes. Every vehicle in our fleet carries comprehensive insurance. Your safety and peace of mind is our top priority on every trip.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, 24/7 airport transfers at Rajiv Gandhi International Airport (HYD). We track your flight and ensure on-time pickup for all flights.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept UPI, NEFT/IMPS, cash, and all major credit/debit cards. Partial advance may be required for luxury vehicle bookings.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Free cancellation up to 12 hours before scheduled pickup. Modifications can be requested anytime via WhatsApp and we'll do our best to accommodate.

Do you serve outside Hyderabad?

Yes! We cover major outstation routes — Tirupati, Srisailam, Bengaluru, Vijayawada, Vizag and more. Contact us for custom routes and pricing.

Is fuel included in the rental?

For chauffeur-driven services, fuel is included. For self-drive, you are responsible for fuel — vehicle provided full, return full.
